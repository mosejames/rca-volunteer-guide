Upload Schedule PDF

AI reads the PDF and extracts the schedule grid into structured data. This works best as a starting point, not a finished product.

The schedule grid is complex. Expect the AI to get the general structure right but miss details on group assignments, split locations, or combined time blocks. Always review the preview table carefully and correct anything that looks off before pushing.

Upload Excel or CSV

Upload the working spreadsheet (.xlsx or .csv). The file should have columns matching this format: day, time_start, time_end, groups, location, note, flag. Column headers are matched automatically. After upload, you can review and edit before pushing to the live sheet.

Create from Template

Creates a new tab in the Google Sheet by copying the TEMPLATE tab. The template has the standard time block skeleton already filled in. Pick the date, click Create, then open the sheet to fill in group assignments and locations.

Preview & Edit

Review the parsed data below. Click any cell to edit it directly. Use the + Add Row button to fill in anything that was missed. When it looks right, pick the date and push to the Google Sheet. The volunteer guide will pick up the new data within 45 seconds.
